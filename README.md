# Number Card Game (6-7)

A fun, interactive card game built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS!

## Game Rules

- Both the player and dealer start with 2 random cards (values 1-10)
- - **Objective**: Try to reach a score of exactly **6 or 7** without going over 10
  - - **Hit**: Draw another card to increase your score
    - - **Stand**: Keep your current score and let the dealer play
      - - Scores over 10 result in a bust (automatic loss)
        - - The player with the best score wins, with bonus points for hitting exactly 6 or 7!
